Our Journey

2000

The Beginning

LifeCare Medical Center was founded as a small clinic with just 10 beds and a team of 5 dedicated healthcare professionals.

2005

Expansion

Expanded to a 50-bed facility with the addition of specialized departments including Cardiology, Pediatrics, and Orthopedics.

2010

Technological Advancement

Implemented electronic health records and acquired state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment to enhance patient care.

2015

Research Center

Established a dedicated research center to advance medical knowledge and develop innovative treatment approaches.

2020

Modern Expansion

Completed a major expansion to a 200-bed facility with specialized centers of excellence and cutting-edge technology.

2023

Digital Transformation

Launched telemedicine services and a patient portal to enhance accessibility and convenience for our patients.
