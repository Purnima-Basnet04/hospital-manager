Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by calling our appointment line at (123) 456-7800, using our online appointment system, or visiting our reception desk in person. We recommend scheduling appointments at least 24 hours in advance for routine visits.

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept most major insurance plans including Medicare, Medicaid, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, Cigna, and UnitedHealthcare. Please contact our billing department at (123) 456-7810 to verify if your specific insurance plan is accepted.

How can I access my medical records?

You can access your medical records through our patient portal. If you haven't registered yet, please visit our reception desk with a valid ID to get your login credentials. For printed copies of your medical records, please submit a written request to our medical records department.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

For your first appointment, please bring your insurance card, a valid photo ID, a list of current medications, your medical history, and any relevant medical records or test results from previous healthcare providers. Arriving 15 minutes early to complete registration forms is recommended.

Do you offer telehealth services?

Yes, we offer telehealth services for certain types of appointments. Please call our appointment line to determine if your medical concern is appropriate for a telehealth visit. Our staff will guide you through the process of setting up a virtual appointment.
